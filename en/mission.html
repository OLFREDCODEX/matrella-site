Mission and research

Exploring the frontiers of social research.

I work on the methodological, technological and organizational changes that shape contemporary social research, with particular attention to evaluation, data quality and the responsible use of digital tools.

Main interests

Three research lines.

  • Methodological innovation New tools, combinations of techniques and attention to data quality.
  • Quality of life of researchers Working conditions, academic trajectories, precarity and organizational well-being.
  • Uses and impacts of digital technologies Digital transformation, research environments, platforms, AI and knowledge production.
Mission

Method as a critical space.

I approach social research methodology not as a fixed set of procedures, but as a critical space for observing, constructing data and interpreting contemporary transformations.

My work focuses on the relationship between methodological rigor, digital environments, new forms of observation and changing conditions of empirical knowledge production.

From research questions to usable evidence.

I collaborate on research design, methodological review, evaluation and data analysis.
