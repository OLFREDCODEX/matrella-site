Quick guide

How to use Methods Guards AI

Methods Guards AI helps read a scientific document from a methodological perspective. The report is not peer review: it is a preliminary diagnosis to identify strengths, weaknesses and possible corrective actions.

Recommended use

1

Prepare the PDF

Use a readable text-based PDF under 19 MB. Scanned PDFs or image-only documents may produce incomplete results.

2

Choose the engine

MG AI v1 is designed for a quick and compact diagnosis. MG AI v2 is more analytical and more attentive to the type of contribution.

3

Read the report

Focus on diagnosis, robustness, main criticisms and corrective actions. The score is an orientation tool, not a final judgment.

4

Check critically

Always compare the report with the original document. If a point is unclear, use it as a revision prompt rather than as an automatic conclusion.

How to interpret the result

The report evaluates only what is visible in the uploaded document: design, sample, instruments, analysis, results, limits and coherence of inferences. If information is not present in the PDF, Methods Guards AI should mark it as not assessable.

A low assessment does not automatically mean that the work has no value. It often means that some methodological information is not documented clearly enough. A high assessment, similarly, does not guarantee editorial acceptance or definitive scientific validity.

Use the report as support for revision: clarify procedures, specify criteria, document limits and make the chain from data to analysis and conclusions more transparent.

What it does not do

  • it does not replace expert human review, peer review or scientific supervision;
  • it does not certify the overall quality of a contribution;
  • it does not guarantee publishability, originality or absence of errors;
  • it should not be used as the only criterion for academic evaluation;
  • it does not interpret external information that is not present in the uploaded document.

After the report

The best way to use Methods Guards AI is to turn its criticisms into a revision checklist: what to clarify, what to document, which limits to make explicit and which inferences should be made more cautious.
