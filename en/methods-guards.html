Preliminary methodological assessment for research texts

        

          Methods Guards AI is a free tool for reading scientific documents and producing
          an orientative methodological diagnosis. It is designed for papers, abstracts,
          book chapters, reviews and research projects uploaded as PDF files.
        

        

          The report helps identify strengths, weaknesses, declared limitations,
          methodological vulnerabilities and priority corrective actions. It does not
          replace peer review, scientific supervision or expert judgement: it provides
          a structured first check before human review.
        

      


      
    
        
  • Methodological structure: objectives, design, sample, instruments, analysis and conclusions.
    • 
        
  • Robustness: internal coherence, transparency, validity, transferability and limits.
    • 
        
  • v1 and v2 engines: v1 favours quick synthesis; v2 provides a more analytical experimental reading.

    FAQ
  


  


  

    
      Does Methods Guards AI replace human expertise?
    

    

      No. Methods Guards AI is a support system for methodological assessment.
      It does not replace peer review, scientific supervision, academic evaluation or human expert judgment.
    

  


  

    
      Does the AI invent information that is not in the PDF?
    

    

      Methods Guards AI is designed to base the analysis exclusively on the uploaded document.
      Missing information should not be inferred or invented.
    

  


  

    
      Are PDFs stored?
    

    

      Yes. Documents may be stored for technical, security,
      abuse monitoring and system improvement purposes.
      PDFs are not sold, published or publicly shared.
    

  


  

    
      Which documents are supported?
    

    

      Methods Guards AI currently supports:
      abstracts, papers, book chapters and research projects in PDF format.
    

  


  

    
      What does "preliminary methodological diagnosis" mean?
    

    

      The output is an initial and orientative assessment
      of the methodological features of the document,
      not a definitive scientific validation.
    

  


  

    
      Why are some document types not available yet?
    

    

      Methods Guards AI is an evolving project.
      Some document types require more robust analysis pipelines,
      specific checks and additional testing before release.
    

  


  

    
      How do codes work?
    

    

      Codes may be assigned as a form of voluntary support for the project.
      Methods Guards AI does not directly sell credits or premium access.
