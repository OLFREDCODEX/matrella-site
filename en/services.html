Research design

Methodological consulting

Support for strengthening coherence between research question, design, sample, tools, analysis and conclusions.

  • Definition of research questions and objectives
  • Research design and sampling strategy
  • Questionnaires, interview guides and coding grids
  • Critical reading of limits and robustness
Data and interpretation

Data analysis and reporting

Support for organizing, reading and presenting quantitative, qualitative and mixed-methods data.

  • Descriptive and exploratory analysis
  • Dataset cleaning and organization
  • Interpretation of results and limitations
  • Tables, charts and operational summaries
  • Decision-oriented reports
